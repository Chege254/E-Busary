Bursary management system

  • November 13, 2017
  • Focwebtech
  • No Comments
  • Uncategorized

When your looking for a bursary management system, you want to acquire a system that works  from a web development company that knows what its doing. At Focweb Technologies we deliver on time and cost effective web solutions, we have a large volume of experience and no project is too big for us. Our E-bursary management system facilitates the seamless application of bursaries online and provides a dynamic portal for the bursary committee to monitor, track, award bursaries and manage applicants data easily.

The main features include:

  • Online applications.
  • Applicants management.
  • Beneficiary management.
  • Bursary funds management.
  • SMS Module; Automatic sms notifications to successful applicants.
  • Automatic awarding of bursary funds based on past data like location and other factors.
